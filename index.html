Akatsuki (暁)

Akatsuki (暁; Literally meaning "Dawn" or "Daybreak") is a criminal organization of S-Class Criminals and Missing-nin and is the most wanted group in all of shinobi world. Their main goal is to collect all of the Tailed Beasts for their plan of world domination.
